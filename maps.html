This is Iceland

Iceland is a place I have been wanting to go for years because of its amazing landscape and unforgettable aroura borealis light shows that occur throughout the year. One day in the future I hope to plan a trip to the area. I would love to go on various hikes throughout the terrain and enjoy life outside of a metropolitan area.

Reykjavik is Iceland's most populated city! If landing internationally, this will likley be your first stop. The city is powered by geothermal energy that is plentiful in the region. While there is a lot of naturescapes to see in Iceland, the main city is an attraction itself full of the country's culture from arts to food.
